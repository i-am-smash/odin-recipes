Cacio e pepe

Description

Cacio e pepe is an Italian pasta dish meaning "cheese and pepper" in several Italian dialects. It is usually a dish made of spaghetti with grated pecorino romano and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 3/4 cups grated Pecorino Romano cheese

Steps

  1. Add water to a large pot and lightly salt. Boil the water.
  2. Cook the spaghetti in the boiling water while stirring occasionally for about twelve minutes or until tender yet firm to the bite. Reserve 1 cup of the pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and black pepper. Cook and stir for 1 to 2 minutes or until fragrant.
  4. Add the spaghetti and the grated Pecorino Romano. Ladle in 1/2 cup of the pasta water and stir until the cheese is melted. Ladle and stir in more pasta water as need until the sauce coats the spaghetti.
